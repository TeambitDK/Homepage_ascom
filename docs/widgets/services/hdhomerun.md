[HDHomerun](https://www.silicondust.com/support/downloads/) - Plug’n’play products for watching live and recorded TV around your home.

Allowed fields: `["channels", "hd"]`.

```yaml
widget:
  type: hdhomerun
  url: http://hdhomerun.host.or.ip
```
