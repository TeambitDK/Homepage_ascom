---

[Netdata](https://github.com/netdata/netdata) - Monitor your servers, containers, and applications, in high-resolution and in real-time!

Allowed fields: `["warnings", "criticals"]`.

```yaml
widget:
  type: Netdata
  url: http://netdata.host.or.ip
```
